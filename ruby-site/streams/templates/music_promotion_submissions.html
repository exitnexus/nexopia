Submitting Promotional Material to Nexopia!


You want to submit your CD, Record, DVD, Book, Comic, or something else for review? Just want to send us a present? Please send it to the address below. A couple of things though.


We receive a lot of stuff. It makes it hard to give the attention to everything that we want to give but we do look at it. That said, we have a small staff of editorial people working to provide our users with the best and most up to date coverage of contemporary culture but not everything can be addressed. We do our best to cover it all but we can't guarantee anything...so don't be upset or take it personally. We don't think you suck...unless you do suck...but we don't really have time to think it anyway so don't worry.


Please describe what you are sending and provide us with a bit of background info. We would like to think that we know the deal and that we can contextualize your product, but it saves us some time to have the info...time is short so help us out.


Don't phone us...you won't get a hold of the editorial department. You can send a polite email to   and ask if we received your submission. We will get back to you as soon as we can and say "Yes we got it" or "We have no idea what you are talking about." We can then figure it all out from there...
