Import contacts from your personal email address book to find out who is already on Nexopia and who should be invited.
More info...
Still can't figure it out? We understand... computers can be irritating. Just enter your email address and your email password into the search boxes below and then press continue at the bottom of the page. What you'll get is a list of all your friends who are already on Nexopia + a bunch of them you should invite.

To guarantee you'll be able to find who you're looking for we've made all of these email services compatible:
  • Hotmail
  • MSN
  • Hotmail Live
  • Yahoo mail
  • Gmail
  • AOL
  • Lycos (including international Lycos.co.uk)
  • .Mac (mac.com)
  • Mail.com (and all its domain variants)
  • FastMail.FM (and all its domain variants)
  • Rediffmail
  • Indiatimes
  • GMX
  • LinkedIn
  • IcqMail
  • Web.de
  • MyNet.com
If you're thinking 'ARGH, my email account isn't on the list' and shaking your fist at the screen, then you should know that it's probably because you're using either Outlook Express or Thunderbird. Breathe. And move onto the second search tool.
